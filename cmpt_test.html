What is the CMPT Profile?
The CMPT Profile is a validated 40-question personality assessment based on four core psychological dimensions. It reveals how you gain energy, how you process information, how you make decisions, and how you organise your life — producing one of 16 personality types.
C
Connection
How you draw and direct your energy — outward or inward
M
Mindset
How you take in information — through facts or through patterns
P
Priorities
How you make decisions — through logic or through values
T
Tactics
How you organise your life — with structure or with flexibility
How to answer
Each question presents two statements. Choose the one that feels most natural to you — not the one you think is "better." There are no right or wrong answers. Answer based on who you genuinely are, not who you aspire to be.
