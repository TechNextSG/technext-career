How It Works

Four steps from link to insight

Candidates complete the profiler in minutes. Results land in Odoo before the recruiter finishes their coffee.

1

Candidate receives their assessment link

HR shares the personalised role URL (e.g. /technext/ai-engineer). The profiler is embedded in the multi-step test sequence alongside the company quiz and technical assessment — no separate login required.

2

32 adaptive questions — DISC then 4-letter Type

The first 16 questions measure work-style dominance across four behavioural dimensions (D, I, S, C). The next 16 map four personality dichotomies to produce a 4-letter type code (e.g. ENTJ). A 6-minute timer is shown — most candidates finish in 4–5 minutes.

3

Instant results page — candidate sees their profile

Primary + secondary DISC letter, four percentage bars, and a plain-English description of the personality type are shown immediately. The candidate can review and continue to the next assessment without refreshing.

4

Automatic sync to Odoo — no manual entry

The moment the candidate clicks Continue, the result is written to Supabase and mirrored to Odoo as a structured chatter note on the candidate's hr.applicant record. DISC scores, type code, primary/secondary letters, and resume link are all captured.

Framework 1 — DISC

Work-style DNA across four dimensions

DISC measures how a person behaves at work — not what they know. Every candidate receives a primary and secondary letter with a percentage score for each dimension.

D

Dominance — The Driver

Results-focused and direct. Thrives on challenge, makes decisions quickly, and pushes hard for outcomes. Can be impatient with slow processes or indecision.

Decisive Bold Competitive Assertive
I

Influence — The Energiser

Outgoing, enthusiastic, and persuasive. Energises teams, builds relationships fast, and excels at pitching ideas. Can lose focus on details or follow-through.

Optimistic Collaborative Persuasive Spontaneous
S

Steadiness — The Stabiliser

Patient, dependable, and team-oriented. Provides the calm backbone of any group, listens deeply, and builds long-term trust. May resist rapid change or conflict.

Reliable Empathetic Patient Supportive
C

Conscientiousness — The Analyst

Detail-oriented, systematic, and quality-driven. Asks the right questions, follows processes rigorously, and produces accurate work. Can over-analyse or become perfectionistic.

Precise Analytical Systematic Quality-focused

Each candidate also receives a combined label — e.g. "Di: The Driver-Influencer" — capturing their primary work style with the secondary dimension as a modifier.

Framework 2 — 4-Letter Type

Cognitive style across four spectrums

The second half of the assessment maps how a person thinks, communicates, and structures their world — producing a 4-letter code from 16 possible types.

E
VS
I

Extraversion vs Introversion

Does the candidate recharge through people and conversation (E) or through solitude and reflection (I)? Critical for remote vs office-fit.

N

Sensing vs iNtuition

Does the candidate focus on concrete facts and present reality (S) or on patterns, possibilities, and big-picture thinking (N)?

T

Thinking vs Feeling

Does the candidate prioritise objective logic and data (T) or team harmony, values, and the human impact of decisions (F)?

J

Judging vs Perceiving

Does the candidate prefer structure, schedules, and closure (J) or flexibility, adaptability, and keeping options open (P)?

Backend Integration

Every result lands in Odoo — automatically

📬 What gets written to Odoo

  • Candidate name, email, phone number
  • Job position (e.g. AI Engineer) linked automatically
  • DISC scores: D%, I%, S%, C% as structured fields
  • Primary + secondary DISC letter (e.g. "D", "i")
  • 4-letter type code (e.g. "ENTJ")
  • LinkedIn profile URL
  • Resume attached directly to the applicant card
  • Supabase row ID for full audit trail

🛡️ How the sync is protected

  • Supabase write succeeds first — Odoo failure never blocks the candidate
  • Hard 3.5 s timeout prevents slow Odoo responses from affecting UX
  • Kill switch via environment variable (ODOO_SYNC_ENABLED=false)
  • Upsert by email — submitting twice updates the same applicant record
  • No PII logged — only email hash + event type in structured logs
  • API key with 1-year expiry, stored securely in Vercel environment
